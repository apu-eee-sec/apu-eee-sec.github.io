My Line Following Robot

Specifications:

  • Chassis: 2-wheel car design
  • Microcontroller: Arduino Nano
  • Sensors: Infrared sensors array for line detection
  • Motors: DC motors with motor driver for precise control
  • Power: Rechargeable LiPo battery

My robot is designed to quickly and accurately follow black lines on various courses. While it may not be the fastest, it's been a reliable companion throughout my competition journey.

Competition Experiences

Regional Robotics Showdown 2023

This was my first competition. The course had several sharp turns and a few tricky intersections. My robot struggled with the speed required but managed to complete the course.

National Line Follower Challenge 2023

The national competition featured a complex course with multiple path options. While my robot took a longer route, it successfully avoided all obstacles and completed the challenge.

University Tech Fest 2024

This competition introduced elevated sections and varying line widths. My robot performed better than previous competitions, showing the improvements I had made to its sensors and control algorithms.

Lessons Learned

  • Importance of sensor calibration for different lighting conditions
  • The need for robust error correction in the control algorithm
  • Value of testing on various surface materials before competition
  • Significance of weight distribution for speed and stability
  • The joy of problem-solving and continuous improvement

While I haven't won any prizes yet, each competition has taught me valuable lessons and fueled my passion for robotics. The experience of building, testing, and improving my line-following robot has been incredibly rewarding.
